 Business Insights
 Executive Summary

The analysis of restaurant data reveals that customer satisfaction is influenced by a combination of pricing, service features, and engagement (votes). While pricing plays a role, features like online ordering and customer interaction significantly impact ratings and popularity.

 Key Findings
1 Pricing vs Customer Satisfaction
Low, medium, and high-cost restaurants show similar rating patterns
Higher price does NOT guarantee better ratings

 Insight:
Customers value experience over price

2 Customer Engagement is Critical
Total votes: 78,384
Restaurants with higher votes tend to have better visibility and trust

 Insight:
Popularity drives perception and success

3 Online Ordering Improves Performance
Restaurants offering online ordering have better average ratings

Insight:
Convenience is a key driver of customer satisfaction

4 Table Booking Has Limited Impact
Mixed results across restaurants
Not a strong factor compared to online ordering

Insight:
Table booking is not a major differentiator

5 Top Restaurants Combine Multiple Factors
High rating + high votes = success
Balance of quality + visibility

Insight:
Success comes from both quality AND customer engagement
 Strategic Recommendations
1. Focus on Customer Experience
Improve food quality and service
Encourage customer reviews
2. Enable Online Ordering
Essential for modern restaurant success
3. Optimize Pricing Strategy
Avoid assuming higher price = higher satisfaction
4. Increase Customer Engagement
Promotions, reviews, feedback systems
Business Impact
Better customer retention
Increased visibility
Improved revenue potential
 Final Conclusion

Restaurants that combine good service, customer engagement, and convenience features outperform others regardless of pricing level.
